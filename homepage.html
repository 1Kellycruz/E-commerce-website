About Us

Abuelitas Authentic Tocas is a premier catering company that specializes in bringing the authentic flavors of Latin American cuisine to any event. With a name that pays homage to the cherished abuelitas, or grandmothers, who are known for their exceptional culinary skills, Abuelitas Authentic Tocas has become a trusted choice for those seeking a truly authentic and memorable catering experience.
