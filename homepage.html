About Us

Abuelas is a catering company that specializes in bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to any event. With a name that pays homage to the admired abuelas, or grandmothers, who are known for their exceptional culinary skills, Abuelas has become a trusted choice for those seeking a truly authentic and memorable catering experience.

What sets Abuelas apart from other catering companies is their commitment to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From handmade tortillas to homemade salsas, everything is prepared from scratch to ensure the highest level of authenticity and flavor.
